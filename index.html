Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about JITSIFY.

When does JITSIFY launch?

We're launching in May 2026 on iOS. Join the waitlist to get early access and be the first to know when we go live.

What is a flow diagram?

A flow diagram is a visual map of a grappling exchange — positions, transitions, and decision points laid out so you can study your game at a glance. Think of it as a game plan you can actually see.

Do I need to be an experienced grappler to use JITSIFY?

Not at all. JITSIFY is built for all levels — from white belts mapping their first techniques to black belts building complex game plans. The AI understands plain English, so just describe what you know.

How does the AI work?

Describe a technique or exchange in your own words, and our AI parses it into a structured flow diagram with positions, transitions, and who's acting. It understands BJJ terminology natively.

Can I annotate my own rolling footage?

Yes. Import any video, tag moves with timestamps and move types, and JITSIFY overlays your flow diagram right on top of the footage. You can also export annotated clips for Instagram.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — the yearly plan includes a 3-day free trial. You can explore everything JITSIFY has to offer before being charged. Cancel anytime.

What platforms is JITSIFY available on?

JITSIFY is launching on iOS first (iPhone and iPad). Android and web versions are on the roadmap based on demand.
